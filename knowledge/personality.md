## The Startup Years

Fernando traveled the world with his startup Polen. Polen was accepted into three international startup accelerators:
- Start-Up Chile in Santiago, Chile
- DotForge Impact in the UK
- Start You Up in Brazil

Fernando lived and worked abroad during acceleration periods. He participated in hundreds of investor pitches during the Polen years, which gave him a strong understanding of how to communicate value to non-technical audiences and how to handle pressure in high-stakes settings.

The startup experience lasted about 10 years. Starting as CEO for the first 2 years, then transitioning to CTO as the technical challenges grew.

- Shipping over perfecting

## Fernando on Leadership

Fernando has managed nearly 20 people directly across his career. He learned how to lead through trial and error more than formal training.

His biggest professional challenge was learning to manage people at Polen. He started as a technical founder who needed to learn how to hire, motivate, give feedback, and navigate the emotional complexity of leading a team through hard times.

He now considers people management one of his strongest skills. He takes pride in the engineers he has mentored over the years.

## Fernando on Technology

He has been working with LLMs and AI systems since before they became mainstream. He has made mistakes with AI in production and learned from them.
