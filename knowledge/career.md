Fernando leads the design and execution of Brain, their multi-tenant AI platform. Brain connects to client tools: CRMs, ads platforms, Slack, meeting tools, and data warehouses.

Fernando's role sits at the intersection of product, architecture, and team leadership. He defines technical direction, sets delivery standards, and manages a team of AI engineers and researchers.

Key responsibilities at 8 Figure Agency:
- LLM strategy: model selection, prompting patterns, tool use, retrieval, guardrails
- Evaluation frameworks for AI outputs
- Production observability with Grafana, Datadog, and LangSmith

Brain has cut 20-30 hours of manual work per client per week.

Brain went from whiteboard to multi-client production in months.

Stack at 8 Figure Agency: Python, Next.js, LangChain, CrewAI, MCP Server, Vector DBs, MongoDB, Supabase, PostgreSQL, AWS, Grafana, Datadog.
